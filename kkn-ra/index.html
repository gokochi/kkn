Welcome to the official page of the Karakulathunadu Residents Association! Our association is dedicated to fostering a strong sense of community, maintaining the natural beauty of our village, and enhancing the quality of life for all residents. Named after our serene paddy fields and the mellow relaxing walkways that grace our landscape, Karakulathunadu is not just a place to live; it’s a way of life.

At Karakulathunadu Residents Association, we are committed to creating a harmonious living environment that encapsulates the essence of our village. Our association was established by a group of enthusiastic individuals who share a deep love for our community and a vision for its growth and prosperity. Our core values include unity, sustainability, and preservation of our cultural heritage.

Our Mission

The Karakulathunadu Residents Association is dedicated to:

  • Community Unity: Fostering a strong sense of togetherness and cooperation among all residents, irrespective of age, background, or beliefs.

  • Preservation of Heritage: Protecting and promoting our cultural and historical heritage, ensuring that the stories of our village are passed down to future generations.

  • Environmental Stewardship: Striving to maintain the natural beauty of our surroundings, including the picturesque paddy fields and serene walkways.

  • Quality of Life Enhancement: Initiating projects and programs that contribute to the overall well-being and happiness of every resident.
