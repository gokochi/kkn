Welcome to Karakulathunadu Tourist Village, an enchanting destination where nature’s beauty, cultural heritage, and warm hospitality come together to create an unforgettable experience.

Our Story

Nestled amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Chalakudy Kerala India, Karakulathunadu is a picturesque village known for its vibrant paddy fields and organic vegetable farming. Our village has a rich history that dates back centuries, and we take immense pride in preserving our cultural traditions and eco-friendly practices.

The Beauty of Paddy Fields Farming

As you step into Karakulathunadu, you will be greeted by the mesmerizing sight of expansive paddy fields that stretch as far as the eye can see. These lush green fields not only paint a picture of serenity but also represent the essence of our agrarian lifestyle. Immerse yourself in the gentle rhythm of rural life as you take leisurely strolls through the fields, witnessing the farmers’ skillful techniques passed down through generations.

Organic Vegetables Farming

At Karakulathunadu, we believe in sustainable agriculture, and our organic vegetable farming is a testament to our commitment to the environment and your well-being. Wander through our bountiful vegetable gardens and witness the careful nurturing of a wide variety of fresh, pesticide-free produce. From crunchy cucumbers to vibrant tomatoes, every bite is a celebration of nature’s goodness.

Graceful Welcoming People

As you explore the beauty of our village, you will encounter the heartwarming hospitality of our people. Our community takes immense pride in extending a warm and genuine welcome to visitors from all walks of life. Engage in conversations with the locals, and you’ll discover the unique customs, folklore, and cultural practices that have been cherished for generations.

A Journey of Enrichment

At Karakulathunadu Tourist Village, we offer more than just a vacation; we invite you on a journey of enrichment. Whether you’re seeking a tranquil retreat from the bustling city life, an opportunity to witness traditional farming practices, or a chance to embrace a different culture, our village promises an experience that will touch your heart and soul.

Your Experience Awaits

Come and be captivated by the beauty of Karakulathunadu Tourist Village. Our doors are open to travelers seeking an authentic and meaningful getaway. Embrace the charm of rural life, indulge in farm-fresh delights, and forge connections with the welcoming people who call this village home.
