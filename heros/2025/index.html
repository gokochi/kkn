KKN Residents Association - Our Heroes of 2025

This page proudly verifies the authenticity of the Community Service Awards presented to our deserving youth at the 2nd Annual Celebration of the KKN Residents Association. The ceremony took place in the distinguished presence of Chalakudy Municipal Chairperson, Shri Shibu Valappan, and Ward Councillor, Shri K.P. Balan, alongside the dedicated KKN Executive Members, including the President, Secretary, and Treasurer.

We extend our deepest gratitude to these young individuals whose selflessness and dedication have profoundly enriched our community.

Award Recipients - 2025 (Alphabetical Order)

  • Anugrah M.S.
  • Hela Krishna M.L.
  • Manudarsh M.
  • Nandana M.A.
  • Nila M.P.
  • Varsha Varghese

This page serves as a lasting tribute to the remarkable contributions of our young community members.
